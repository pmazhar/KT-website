Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Koja Travels respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

We may collect information such as your account information, email address, phone number, selected citizenships, travel preferences, location data, and platform usage data in order to provide and improve our services.

Location information may be used to power maps, destination discovery features, travel recommendations, and other location-based functionality within the platform.

Koja Travels does not collect passport numbers, passport scans, or government-issued identification documents.

Information collected may be used to personalize your experience, support platform functionality, communicate important updates, improve our services, and maintain account security.

Users may request deletion of their account and associated personal data at any time through the app or by contacting hello@kojatravels.com. We will make reasonable efforts to remove personal data from our active systems within 30 days of a verified deletion request, unless retention is required by law.

We take reasonable measures to protect user information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@kojatravels.com.
