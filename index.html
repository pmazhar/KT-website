About Koja

Koja is a social travel discovery and planning platform that helps travelers uncover destinations, coordinate trips with friends, and prepare for their next life-changing adventure.

We created Koja because we love travel and believe discovering the world should feel exciting—not overwhelming.

Today's travelers are surrounded by endless content, conflicting recommendations, and more information than ever before. Finding the right destination, planning a trip with friends, and understanding where to go next can quickly become exhausting.

Our mission is to make travel discovery and planning simpler, more trustworthy, and more accessible. By bringing together recommendations, destination insights, and collaborative trip planning in one place, Koja helps travelers spend less time searching and more time exploring.

Whether you're planning a weekend getaway or your next life-changing adventure, we want to help you travel with confidence using information and recommendations from sources you trust.

Koja Travels is launching soon on iOS, with Android support planned for a future release.
